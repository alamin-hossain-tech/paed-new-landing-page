What do you need help with?

Ear, nose, and throat (ENT)

Ear, nose, and throat (ENT) issues are common in children and can have a significant impact on their daily lives and overall development.
- Allergic rhinitis (nasal allergies)
- Ear blockage and infections
- Hearing problems
- Snoring
- Sinnusitis
- Tonsillitis

Plastic surgery

Plastic surgery focuses on both the functional and aesthetic needs of growing children, ensuring that their physical appearance and overall health develop as naturally as possible.
- Ear deformities
- Moles, cysts and tumours
- Scars from burns, injuries or surgeries

Urgent Care

- Animal or human bites
- Allergy reactions
- Bleeding or burn wounds
- Breathing difficulties/Shortness of breath
- Chest pain
- Fever in children below 3 months old
- Fractures or broken bones
- Seizures

Orthopaedics

Orthopaedics focuses on diagnosing, treating, and managing musculoskeletal issues in children.
- Bow-legged knees
- Clubfoot
- Developmental dysplasia of the hip
- Fractures
- Flat feet
- Scoliosis
- Sprains or strains

Eye (Ophthalmology)

Children's developing vision requires special care, as early detection and treatment of eye issues are crucial for preventing long-term visual impairments.
- Droopy eyelid
- In-turned eyelashes
- Lazy Eye
- Refractive errors (eg. myopia, astigmatism)
- Squinting

General Care

- Constipation
- Cough, runny nose, sore throat
- HFMD (Hand, foot, mouth, disease)
- Mild vomiting and/or diarrhoea
- Fever in children above 3 months old
- Vaccination
- Weight management

Dermatology

Paediatric dermatology focuses on diagnosing and treating skin conditions in infants, children, and adolescents.
- Eczema
- Fungal infections
- Hives
- Skin infection
- Warts
