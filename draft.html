Ear, nose, and throat (ENT)

Ear, nose, and throat (ENT) issues are common in children and can have a significant impact on their daily lives and overall development.
- Allergic rhinitis (nasal allergies)
- Ear blockage and infections
- Hearing problems
- Snoring
- Sinnusitis
- Tonsillitis
