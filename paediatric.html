He previously served as head of the children’s emergency department at a public hospital in Singapore for 5 years. Dr Lee is highly experienced in paediatric emergency medicine, having practiced in this specialty for over 12 years.

Our paediatric clinic cares for children, from newborns through their early teen years. We provide comprehensive quality care from diagnosis through to treatment and rehabilitation.

A one-stop centre with multidisciplinary services including general practitioners and family physicians services, extended-hours urgent care, and specialists such as our paediatrician, Dr Irene Teo.

With over 38 clinics around Singapore, our general practitioners and family physicians can provide regular check-ups, child vaccinations, and age appropriate screenings to ensure your little ones stay healthy and on track with their development, from infancy through adolescence.
